# Abstract

We analyze historical archive of SEU on Inmarsat, a mobile communication satellite. Inmarsat is in GEO and collected extensive satellite anaomly and telemtry data since 1990. over 3 decades it has seen 226 SEUs. The goal of paper is to correlate occurences to space weather phoenomena such as solar proton evetns (SPE) that originate from coronal mass ejections CMEs. SPEs are most prevalent at solar maximum and SEU on Inmarsats satellites occur out of phase suggesting SPEs are not prmary cause

# 1. Introduction

Inmarasat is a UK satellite telecommuncation company with 10 operating satellites for global coverage. Inmarsats database includes component health data  - total bus power,solar array current, records of various anomolis fof solid state power amplifier (SSPA) failures, telemetry corruption, and SEU.

Satellite fleets are ferred to as $A$ and $B$ in paper. fleet $A$ has seen 28 SEU with average 5.6 SEU/satellite and $B$ has seen 198 with average of 66 SEU/satellite.

Particles greater than 10 MeV are capable of penetrating surface of a satellite. main sources are solar energetic protons (SPE) and galactic cosmic rays (GCRs)

High-energy solar protons originate from coronal mass ejections (CME). particles take 1.5-2 days to reach earth with speeds of 1000 km/s. they cause solar array degradation.

High energy protons can penetrate Earths magnetic field at poles, crash into neutral atmospheric particles and produce ion- electron pairs that temporarily increase the plasma density in the lowest region of the ionosphere causing absorption of shart wave radio signals and widepread blackout of communtions. This is sometimes called a polar cap abosroption event. High energy particle radiation build up in the magnetopshere; this radiation can damage spacecraft mircroelectronics.

GCR consists mostly of protons with $\alpha$ particles. Radiaiton form GCR peaks at solar minimum and valleys at solar maximum due to solar winds not interfering with the GCR path. GCR will not be talked about in study

SEU in Inmarsat is a function of hardware. both $A$ adn $B$ fleets have similar design, multiple copies can very 15%

Inmarsats uses automatic on-board error detction (EDAC?) with automated ground system monitoring

# 2. Solar Proton Events

SPEs excite protons and heavy ions to high energy levels capable of penetrating surrounding strucutres and shielding of satellite electronics. energy level penetration around 50-100 MeV/nucleon. 10 MeV can also contribute to SUEs

SPE data from NOAA Space Weather Prediciton Center (SWPC). Proton fluxes integrate over 5 minute averages recroded from Geostationary Operational Enviromental Satellites (GOES). 

Start of proton event defined when three consecutive proton fluxes are recorded at a rate greater than or equal to 10 particle flux units (pfu). End of event defined is last instance of rate above 10 pfu

Compare occurnece of SPE to solar cycle.  Number of sunpsots is correlated to strength and flucuation of solar acitivty like flares and CMEs. Even at solar minimum the sun can produce damaging storms. solar magnetic activiy scyle has period of 11 years


a significant event is spe with 10 MeV protons at rate greater than 10 pfu. The most feuqnet and strongest SPE occur when sunspot number at maximum (solar maximum).

Bartels Cycles: similar to Carrington cycle, is the 27 day cycle of the Suns rotation. SUE occcur o nthe 3rd and 26th day of the Bartels cycle.

# 3. Single Event Upsets

Relationship between high-energy protons and SEUs has been studied since 1979. Articles:
* Guenzer et al
* McNulty et al
Both presented evidence that high energy protons were capable of causing SEUs. Follow up study with NASA Tracking Delay Relay Satellite System (TDRS-1) from 1984 to 1990 - Wilkinson D 1991. Wilkinson finds that protons with energies greater than 10-40 MeV tend to induce SEUs. Most likely to occur when >50 MeV with rate of 10 pfu.

Solar wind composition: solar plasma, ionized hydrogen mainly. work for alpha particles and heavy ions for future work

Majority of satellite anomalies occur in spring and fall. no trends with Bartels cycle.

More SPEs occur at solar maximum than at solar minimum. SEUs tend to occur most often at solar minimum. Inverse relationship strongly suggests that SEUs were not primarly caused by  solar particle events SEP. The more SPE occur at solar maximum the more likely source of the SEUS could be GCRs which occur at solar minimum

See paper for specfici results and timing of SPEs and SEUs

# Conclusion

SEU on the Inmarsat fleet were not found to correlate to solar proton events. No clear correaltion between SEUs and the age of the satellite. Next step is investigating the correlation between SEUs and GCRs as a function of the 11 year solar cycle
