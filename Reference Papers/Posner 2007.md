## 4.2 Characterizing Initial Increase in Particle Intensity With Statistical and Superposed Epoch Techniques

During onset period we examine semi log plots of onset electron and protons and use a regression fit line to find the slope (increase parameter) to tell us characterisitics of the event.

To examine the rise paramters we use three methods

1. Onset- to - Break method: best estimate for onset of event and subsequent break in intensity increase
2. Center interval of onset to break method. AFter method 1 the sub interval in its center with exactly half the interval duration is selected. increase paramter is derived from straigh line fit. Accuracy suffers for proton onsets
3. Stepped 10 min increase method. Fixed 10 minute interval straight line fits. largest increase paramter is selected for any event
Using method we establush new parameters : $\phi_e, \phi_p$ for all solar energetic particle events

$$
\begin{align}
\phi_e &= \frac{d}{d t}\left[\log_{10} \left(\frac{I_{e, t= t_2}}{I_{e, t = t_1}}\right) \right]\\\\
\phi_p &= \frac{d}{d t}\left[\log_{10} \left(\frac{I_{p, t= t_2}}{I_{p, t = t_1}}\right) \right]
\end{align}
$$
